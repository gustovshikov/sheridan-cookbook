# Smoked Pork Tenderloin

**Prep time:** 15-30 mins | **Smoke time:** About 1 hr | **Temp:** 225°F

### Ingredients

- 1-2 Pork tenderloins
- 1-2 Tbsp Yellow mustard or olive oil
- 2-3 Tbsp BBQ rub
- BBQ sauce
- _Optional:_ Kosher salt and black pepper, if your rub is salt-free

### Instructions

1. **Trim:** Remove the silverskin and any loose fat from the pork tenderloin.
2. **Bind & Season:** Coat lightly with mustard or olive oil, then season all sides with BBQ rub. Rest 15 to 30 minutes while the smoker heats.
3. **Smoke:** Preheat the smoker to 225°F. Smoke until the thickest part reaches 135°F.
4. **Sauce:** Brush with BBQ sauce, then continue smoking until the internal temperature reaches 145°F.
5. **Rest:** Rest 5 to 10 minutes before slicing.
