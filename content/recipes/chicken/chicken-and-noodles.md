# Chicken and Noodles

### Ingredients

- 1 whole chicken
- 1 envelope Lipton soup mix, onion/mushroom or vegetable
- Lemon pepper, garlic, chicken seasoning, or Italian seasoning
- Dumpling noodles or wide noodles
- 1-2 cups water, for noodles

### Instructions

1. Remove the gizzards, heart, and anything else from the chicken cavity. Wash the chicken with cold water, no soap.
2. Fill a roast pan half full of water and add the soup mix.
3. Place the chicken in the mixture, making sure the empty chicken cavity fills with liquid.
4. Sprinkle the top of the chicken with one of the seasonings.
5. Bake at 350°F for 4 hours.
6. Remove the chicken from the pan after taking it out of the oven. Save all the liquid in the pan for noodles.
7. Add 1 to 2 cups water to the liquid in the roast pan.
8. Bring to a boil.
9. Add dumpling noodles or wide noodles to the boiling liquid and cook according to package directions.
10. When the noodles reach the desired texture, remove them from the liquid in the pan.
11. Refrigerate leftover chicken. Debone if desired.
12. Drain the noodles; do not keep the liquid mixture.
