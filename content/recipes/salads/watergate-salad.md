# Watergate Salad

### Ingredients

- 1 can crushed pineapple, 13 oz
- 9 oz Cool Whip, or more
- 1/2 cup miniature marshmallows
- 1 package instant pistachio pudding, 3 oz

### Instructions

1. Put crushed pineapple, juice included, into a bowl.
2. Sprinkle pudding mix over pineapple and mix.
3. Mix in marshmallows and Cool Whip.
4. Refrigerate for a few hours before serving.
5. Add a bit more Cool Whip before serving, if desired.

### Notes

- The scan calls this "Green Stuff."
- This makes a small amount. Increase the recipe amounts if needed.
