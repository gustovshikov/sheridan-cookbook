# 6-Can Soup

### Ingredients

- 1 can corn, do not drain
- 1 jar salsa, 12 oz or less depending how spicy you want the soup
- 1 can refried beans
- 1 can black beans, drained
- 1 can chicken broth
- 1 can stewed tomatoes with garlic and onion
- 1 can chicken chunks

### Instructions

1. Heat until mixed together and hot.
2. Serve with shredded cheese, sour cream, and tortilla chips.
