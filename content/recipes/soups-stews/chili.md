# Chili

### Ingredients

- 1 lb lean hamburger
- Lemon pepper
- Dried onion
- Chili powder
- 2-3 cans tomato sauce
- Pinto beans, kidney beans, or red beans, optional

### Instructions

1. Brown hamburger seasoned with dried onion and lemon pepper.
2. Drain grease from the hamburger after it is cooked. Do not put grease down the kitchen drain.
3. Add tomato sauce to the texture you want.
4. Add 1/4 teaspoon or more chili powder.
5. Add beans if using.
