# Beef Barley Soup

### Ingredients

- Medium Quaker barley, cooked according to the water amount on the box
- Browned hamburger
- Onion
- Lemon pepper
- 2 cans diced tomatoes
- 1 can hominy, drained
- 1 can cut green beans, drained
- 1 beef bouillon
- Water, if needed

### Instructions

1. Cook the medium Quaker barley according to the back of the box for water amount.
2. Brown the hamburger with onion and lemon pepper, then drain.
3. Add the diced tomatoes, drained hominy, drained green beans, and beef bouillon to the barley.
4. Add water if it is not soupy enough.
5. Cook until it reaches the texture you want.
