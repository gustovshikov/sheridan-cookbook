# Oat Bran Tortillas

### Ingredients

- 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
- 1 cup plus 2 tablespoons oat bran
- 1 3/4 teaspoons baking powder
- 1/8 teaspoon salt
- 1 cup plus 2 tablespoons boiling water

### Instructions

1. In a large bowl, stir together flour, oat bran, baking powder, and salt.
2. Gradually stir in boiling water to form a soft dough.
3. Knead on a lightly floured surface for about 5 minutes, then cover and let rest for 10 minutes.
4. Divide dough into 18 golf-ball-sized portions and roll each into a ball.
5. Flatten each ball with a rolling pin or tortilla press to about 4 inches in diameter and stack them to keep from drying out.
6. Heat a comal, griddle, or large frying pan over medium heat until hot.
7. Roll out one tortilla at a time until paper-thin or as large as your skillet.
8. Cook over medium heat until bubbles form on top.
9. Turn over and cook the other side until starting to brown.
10. Stack cooked tortillas while making the others, taking care not to overcook.
