# No-Knead Maslin Bread

**Prep time:** 10 mins + 18-24 hrs cold ferment | **Bake time:** 40-45 mins | **Temp:** 425°F

### Ingredients

- 300 g Bread flour, preferably King Arthur Bread Flour
- 150 g Einkorn flour, preferably Jovial
- 50 g Whole wheat or rye flour, depending on availability
- 390 g Cool water
- 12.5 g Fine sea salt
- 10 g Honey or molasses
- 1.25-2 g Instant yeast, about a scant 1/2 tsp

### Instructions

1. **Mix:** Dissolve the honey or molasses in the water. Stir in the yeast, flours, and salt until no dry pockets remain.
2. **Rest & Fold:** Cover for 30 minutes, then fold the dough over itself 4-8 times with a wet hand.
3. **Cold Ferment:** Cover tightly and refrigerate 18 to 24 hours.
4. **Shape:** Grease loaf pan(s). Divide if making multiple loaves, shape each piece, and place seam-side down in the pan.
5. **Proof:** Cover and rise at room temperature for 2 to 3 hours, until the dough crests near the rim.
6. **Bake:** Bake at 425°F for 40 to 45 minutes, until deeply browned and 200°F-210°F inside. Cool at least 1 hour.

### Notes

- Wheat and rye are interchangeable in the 10% texture-flour slot. Rye makes a darker, stickier loaf; wheat makes a lighter one.
- Use wet or oiled hands instead of adding lots of flour. Einkorn dough is naturally sticky.
- For freezer loaves, par-bake at 375°F for 30 to 35 minutes, until pale and 180°F-190°F inside. Cool completely, wrap, and freeze. Finish thawed loaves at 425°F for 10 to 15 minutes.
