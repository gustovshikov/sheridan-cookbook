# Banana Bread

### Ingredients

- 2-3 large ripe bananas
- 1/2 cup butter, tub butter
- 2 eggs
- 1 1/2 cups white sugar
- 1/2 cup sour milk, made with 1 tablespoon white vinegar added to regular milk

### Dry Ingredients

- 2 cups flour
- 1 teaspoon baking soda
- 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
- 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
- 1/2 cup chopped nuts, optional

### Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
2. Grease 2 medium loaf pans.
3. Mix the bananas, butter, eggs, sugar, and sour milk in order.
4. Mix in the flour, baking soda, baking powder, cinnamon, and optional nuts.
5. Bake for 1 hour.
6. Let cool in the loaf pans no longer than 5 minutes, then turn pans upside down to remove.
7. Place loaves right side up on a rack to cool. If the bottoms do not get air, they will get soggy.
8. Optionally sprinkle loaves with powdered sugar.
