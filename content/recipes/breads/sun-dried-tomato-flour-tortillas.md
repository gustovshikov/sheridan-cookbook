# Sun-Dried Tomato Flour Tortillas

### Ingredients

- 4 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
- 3 teaspoons baking powder
- 3 teaspoons salt
- 1 package sun-dried tomato pasta sauce mix, dried
- 3/4 cup shortening
- 1 1/8 cups hot water

### Instructions

1. Combine flour, baking powder, salt, and optional dried pasta sauce mix.
2. Cut in shortening by hand or with a pastry cutter until the mixture is crumbly.
3. If the mixture looks more floury than crumbly, add one or two more tablespoons of shortening.
4. Add about 3/4 cup hot water, or just enough to make the ingredients look moist.
5. Knead the mixture with your hand or a large fork, rubbing the dough against the sides of the bowl to gather any clinging dough.
6. If the dough still sticks to the side of the bowl, add a couple more tablespoons of flour until it forms a soft round shape.
7. Cover with a dish towel and let rest for about 1 hour.
8. Pull dough apart into 10 to 12 balls.
9. Lightly flour the rolling area and roll each ball with a rolling pin to about 1/8 inch thick.
10. Place each tortilla on a medium-hot comal, griddle, or skillet.
11. Cook for about 1 to 2 minutes on each side or until the tortilla does not look doughy.
