# Whole Wheat Flour Tortillas

### Ingredients

- 1 cup all-purpose flour
- 4 cups whole wheat bread flour
- 2 teaspoons salt
- 1/2 cup shortening
- 1 1/2 cups boiling water
- All-purpose flour, for rolling

### Instructions

1. In a large bowl, stir together all-purpose flour, whole wheat flour, and salt.
2. Rub in shortening by hand until the mixture is the texture of oatmeal.
3. Make a well in the center and pour in boiling water.
4. Mix with a fork until all water is evenly incorporated.
5. Sprinkle with a bit of additional flour and knead until the dough does not stick to your fingers. The dough should be smooth.
6. Make 18 golf-ball-sized balls, about 2 ounces each.
7. Place on a tray, cover with a cloth, and let stand at least 1 hour or up to 8 hours.
8. Heat a comal, griddle, or large frying pan over high heat.
9. On a lightly floured surface, roll out a tortilla to preferred thinness.
10. Fry one at a time. Place on the griddle for 10 seconds, then flip as soon as bubbles appear on top.
11. Cook about 30 seconds, flip, and cook the other side about 30 seconds.
12. Repeat until all tortillas are cooked.

### Notes

- Tortillas can be refrigerated or frozen.
