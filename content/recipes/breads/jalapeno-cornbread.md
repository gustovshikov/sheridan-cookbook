# Jalapeno Cornbread

### Ingredients

- 1 package jalapeno cornbread mix
- 1 package regular cornbread mix
- 1 lb or more hamburger meat
- Shredded cheddar cheese, to taste

### Instructions

1. Prepare the jalapeno cornbread mix and regular cornbread mix together according to the package directions.
2. Brown the hamburger meat in a skillet and season to taste.
3. Pour half of the cornbread mix into a 9x13 cake pan or dish.
4. Sprinkle the hamburger meat on top of the cornbread batter.
5. Sprinkle shredded cheddar cheese to taste on top of the hamburger.
6. Pour the remaining cornbread batter over the hamburger and cheese.
7. Bake at 325°F for about 40 to 45 minutes. Check to be sure it does not overcook.
