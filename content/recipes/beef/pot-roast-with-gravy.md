# Pot Roast with Gravy

### Ingredients

- Beef roast; shoulder, rump, or chuck roast
- Lemon pepper
- 1 envelope Lipton onion/mushroom soup mix
- 1 onion, sliced
- Parsley flakes

### Gravy

- Flour
- Tap water
- Liquid from the roast

### Instructions

1. Add lemon pepper to the meat, then tenderize by deeply poking it with a knife on both sides to work the seasoning in.
2. Fill a roast pan half full of water and mix in the soup mix.
3. Place meat in the pan with the soup mix.
4. Place separated onion slices on top of the roast and add more lemon pepper and parsley flakes.
5. Cook at 350°F for 3 to 4 hours.

### Gravy Instructions

1. In a cup, mix flour and tap water until runny and not lumpy.
2. Boil the liquid mixture from the roast.
3. Slowly stir the flour mixture into the roast liquid with a whisk.
4. Boil until thickened, stirring frequently so it will not burn.
5. Turn temperature down to low until ready to serve.
