# Roast Beef Hash

### Ingredients

- Leftover roast beef, potatoes, and gravy

### Instructions

1. Pull the roast beef meat apart, cleaning out any fat and bone.
2. Mix the mashed potatoes, gravy, and roast meat together.
3. Heat in the microwave.
