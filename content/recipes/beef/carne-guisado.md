# Carne Guisado

### Ingredients

- 3 lb lean beef, cubed about 1/2 inch
- 1/2 bell pepper, chopped
- 1/4 onion, chopped
- 3/4 teaspoon ground cumin
- Dash of garlic salt or ground garlic
- 2 teaspoons lemon juice
- Salt
- Pepper
- 3 heaping tablespoons shortening
- About 1/4 cup flour

### Instructions

1. Place meat in a large skillet or pot and cover well with water.
2. Cook uncovered over medium-low heat until the water is gone but the pot is not dry, about 1 to 1 1/2 hours.
3. Remove meat chunks, leaving the meat residue in the pot.
4. Add the shortening to the residue and increase heat slightly.
5. When shortening has melted, add enough flour to make a liquid paste, then brown it.
6. Remove about half of the browned flour mixture and save it in a cup for later.
7. Return heat to medium-low.
8. Add the meat, bell pepper, onion, cumin, garlic salt or ground garlic, lemon juice, 1/4 teaspoon salt, 1/4 teaspoon pepper, and enough water to cover the contents.
9. Cook uncovered until some water evaporates and the gravy thickens, about 1 to 1 1/2 hours.
10. About halfway through cooking, taste and add salt and pepper to taste.
11. If the gravy does not thicken properly, add the reserved browned flour mixture about 1 tablespoon at a time.
12. When done, reduce heat to warm.
13. Serve with hot tortillas as soft tacos, or as the main dish with rice and beans.

### Notes

- The first cooking of the meat can be done ahead of time and refrigerated.
