# Sausage Balls

### Ingredients

- 1 lb shredded sharp cheddar or mild cheddar cheese, to taste
- 1 lb sausage meat, such as Jimmy Dean mild
- 3 1/2 cups Bisquick mix

### Instructions

1. Mix ingredients and roll into small balls.
2. Place on a cookie sheet about 1 inch apart.
3. Bake at 400°F for 12 to 15 minutes.
