# Carrot Cake

### Ingredients

- 1 1/2 cups Wesson vegetable oil, do not use other kinds of oil
- 2 cups sugar
- 4 eggs, well beaten
- 2 cups flour
- 2 teaspoons cinnamon
- 2 teaspoons soda
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 3 cups grated carrots

### Icing

- 8 oz cream cheese
- 1 stick oleo or butter, 1/2 cup
- 1 box powdered sugar, 16 oz

### Instructions

1. Mix oil and sugar together.
2. Add well-beaten eggs.
3. Mix dry ingredients together, then add to the above.
4. Add carrots last.
5. Bake at 350°F for approximately 45 minutes or until done.

### Icing Instructions

1. Mix icing ingredients together until creamy. It is best to use an electric beater.
2. Or, use a can of already made creamy butter frosting.

### Notes

- The scan says whole carrots are best: wash, scrape, and grate them rather than buying already grated carrots.
