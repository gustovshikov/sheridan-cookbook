# Peach Cobbler

### Ingredients

- 1 stick oleo or butter, 1/2 cup
- 1 cup flour
- 1 cup sugar
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
- 3/4 cup milk
- 2 cups canned fruit with juice, sliced peaches preferred

### Instructions

1. Melt butter in a baking dish.
2. In a separate bowl, combine flour, sugar, salt, and baking powder.
3. Add milk to the dry mixture.
4. Pour the mixture over the melted butter. Do not stir.
5. Add canned fruit with juice over the mixture. Do not stir.
6. When pouring mixture and fruit, pour lightly over the entire baking dish.
7. Bake at 325°F for approximately 1 hour.

### Notes

- The scan notes this is for a 9x13 pan.
