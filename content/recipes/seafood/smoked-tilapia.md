# Smoked Tilapia

**Prep time:** 1-2 hrs | **Smoke time:** 45-90 mins | **Temp:** 180°F-200°F for fillets, up to 225°F for whole fish

### Ingredients

- Tilapia fillets or whole tilapia, scaled and gutted
- 1 quart Cold water
- 1/4 cup Kosher salt
- 1/4 cup Brown sugar
- _Optional:_ 1/2 tsp Black pepper
- _Optional:_ 1 Smashed garlic clove
- _Optional:_ Lemon slices or 1 bay leaf
- Neutral oil for the grates or fish
- Lemon wedges for serving

### Instructions

1. **Brine:** Mix the water, kosher salt, brown sugar, and any optional aromatics. Brine fillets for 30 to 60 minutes, or whole tilapia for 2 to 4 hours.
2. **Rinse & Dry:** Rinse quickly under cold water and pat very dry.
3. **Form the Pellicle (30-60 mins):** Place the fish on a rack, uncovered in the fridge, until the surface feels slightly tacky.
4. **Prep the Smoker:** Heat the smoker to 180°F-200°F for fillets or up to 225°F for whole fish. Use a mild wood like apple, cherry, or a small amount of pecan.
5. **Smoke:** Lightly oil the grates or use a perforated grill tray. Smoke fillets for 45 to 90 minutes, or whole fish for 1 1/2 to 2 1/2 hours.
6. **Finish:** Pull the fish when the thickest part reaches 145°F and flakes easily.
7. **Rest:** Rest 5 minutes, then finish with lemon right before serving.
