# Corn Pudding

### Ingredients

- 1 can whole corn, do not drain
- 2 eggs
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 2 tablespoons sugar
- 2 tablespoons melted oleo or butter
- 1/2 cup shredded or grated cheddar cheese
- Salt and pepper, to taste

### Instructions

1. Melt butter in a small cake dish or pan.
2. Stir in the corn.
3. Stir the eggs a bit, then stir them into the corn and butter.
4. Stir in flour and sugar. It can be a little lumpy.
5. Stir in salt and pepper.
6. Sprinkle cheese on top. Add more cheese if needed to cover the mixture, but do not stir it into the corn mix.
7. Bake at 350°F for about 1 hour.
