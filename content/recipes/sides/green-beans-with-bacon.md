# Green Beans with Bacon

### Ingredients

- 2 cans cut green beans
- 1/3 onion or medium onion, sweet onion or 1015 onion
- 2 tablespoons butter
- Lemon pepper
- Parsley
- 6-7 slices bacon, pre-cooked or regular

### Instructions

1. Chop the onion and cut up the bacon.
2. Slowly cook bacon in a pan large enough to hold 2 cans of beans.
3. After the bacon is cooked, move it to the edges of the pan.
4. Put butter in the middle of the pan.
5. Mash chopped onions down on top of the butter and add seasoning to the top of the onions.
6. Cook until the onions are soft and usually turn yellow. Stir the bacon and onion mixture separately as needed to keep it from burning.
7. When the onions are soft, stir the bacon and onions together, adding a little water.
8. Drain the green beans and rinse with cold water.
9. Add green beans to the mixture and cook 15 to 20 minutes longer. Green beans usually darken in color when done.
