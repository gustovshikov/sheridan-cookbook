# Mashed Potatoes

### Ingredients

- Potatoes
- Water
- Milk
- Butter

### Instructions

1. Peel and cut the potatoes.
2. Cover with water.
3. Boil until tender.
4. Drain the water from the potatoes, being careful not to burn yourself with the steam.
5. Mash the potatoes and add milk until you get the texture you want.
6. Add butter to taste.
